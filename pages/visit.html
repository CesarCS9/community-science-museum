Visit the museum

Location

The Community Science Museum is located in the city centre of Oslo, with easy access by public transport and nearby facilities for families and school groups.

Galileo Avenue 8 - 0001 Oslo, Norway

How to get there

Public transport

Easily accessible by metro, tram, and bus. The museum is a short walk from nearby public transport stops in central Oslo

Parking

Limited street parking is available nearby. Several public parking garages can be found within walking distance of the museum

School groups

A designated drop-off area is available for school groups and guided visits, making arrival safe and convenient

Services & facilities

  • Enjoy food and drinks during your visit in a our Cafe

  • Storage available for coats and bags

  • Guided tours available for schools and groups

  • Fully wheelchair accessible with accessible restrooms

  • Take home science-themed gifts and souvenirs from our shop

Opening hours

Tuesday-Thursday: 10:00-16:00

Friday: 10:00-19:00

Saturday-Sunday: 09:00-16:00

Closed on Mondays
