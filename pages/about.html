Our mission

The Community Science Museum was created to inspire interest in science and technology through interactive and hands-on learning experiences.

We aim to make science accessible to children, families, and the wider community.

Learning through exploration

We believe children learn best through play, experimentation, and discovery. Our exhibitions and activities are designed to encourage curiosity, creativity, and confidence.
