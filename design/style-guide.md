# Style Guide – Community Science Museum

## 1. Typography

### Logo
- **Font:** Righteous  

### Headings
- **Font:** Lexend  

### Body Text
- **Font:** Questrial  


## 2. Colour Palette

### Primary Colours
- **#FFFFFF** – Main light background  
- **#15173D** – Main dark background  

### Text Colours
- **#000000** – Primary text colour  
- **#FFFFFF** – Text on dark backgrounds  

### Accent Colours
- **#FF6B6B** – Highlighted keywords, brand emphasis, selected CTAs  
- **#FEB21A** – Secondary CTA colour  

### Supporting Colours
- **#4B88A2** – Section backgrounds  
- **#FDF0D5** – Card backgrounds and soft content areas  


## 3. Visual Identity Principles

- Strong keyword-based hero titles to create impact
- Playful yet modern aesthetic to appeal to children and families
